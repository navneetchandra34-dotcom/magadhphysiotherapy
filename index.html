How We Heal

Advanced Treatment Modalities

We do not rely on painkillers. We utilize international-standard physical therapy protocols to correct biomechanics and stimulate cellular repair.

Electrotherapy (आधुनिक मशीनें)

Using targeted modalities like IFT, TENS, and Ultrasound to immediately reduce pain and muscular inflammation.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on joint mobilization and soft tissue manipulation to restore range of motion and break down scar tissue.

Spinal Decompression

Specialized techniques to relieve pressure on compressed spinal nerves, crucial for Sciatica and Slip Disc recovery.

Therapeutic Exercise

Customized stretching and strengthening protocols designed to rebuild core stability and prevent future injuries.

Dry Needling & Cupping

Advanced myofascial release techniques to treat stubborn muscular knots and chronic tension instantly.

Biomechanics Training

Postural correction and ergonomic training to ensure that once you heal, you do not relapse.
